About

The origin of B.L.A.C.C. Connect lies in Los Angeles, California; this in turn reflects the expansion of B.L.A.C.C. Connect and development within other states. With the cooperation of the Blacks within the United States, the development and operation of B.L.A.C.C. Connect will reach its true potential. B.L.A.C.C. Connect requires collaborative work on all levels from small businesses, nonprofit organizations, for profit businesses, resource centers, and all the alike and similar to truly understand the overall standing of the Black Communities.
